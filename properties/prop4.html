Property Description

Price: £900,000

Property type: House

Bedrooms: 3

Location: Hatley Road, Finsbury Park

Beyond the smart, charcoal grey door, the front entrance reveals an impressive hallway with tall ceilings and handy under-stair cupboard. Awash with natural light, the double reception boasts a floating picture ledge and contemporary matte grey painted walls. This versatile double space can easily be utilised for a children’s playroom or spacious home office, for example. Brilliant white cabinets draw the eye upon entrance to the kitchen, boasting a welcoming generosity of storage space and a beautifully integrated range of appliances. Housed within a conservatory-style extension, the additional dining room sports French doors opening onto the timber-decked rear garden.
